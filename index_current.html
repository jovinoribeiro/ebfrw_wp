Who we are

a running club. nonprofit.
located in the San Francisco/Oakland Bay area.

Our Motto: Exercise. Be Healthy. Have Fun!

Our Mission: Promote running and walking for the LGBTA community. ANYONE is welcome.

Where do we meet? On the east side of San Francisco Bay every Saturday morning at 9:00 AM. Distances range between 3-7 miles for runners and 2-4 miles for walkers.

SATURDAY RUNS

Our running and walking events are non-competitive and self-paced. Runners complete between 3 to 7 miles, and walkers 2 to 4 miles. It usually lasts 45 minutes to one hour. All paths and trails are easily accessible to runners and walkers alike.

Pride Run

Our annual fundraising 10K run, 5K run, 5K walk race event.

When? October 14th, 2017 at 9AM

Where? At the beautiful Lake Merritt in downtown Oakland

Club Members, volunteers, sponsors and runners like you are the elements that makes this such a special event. Funds raised are distributed amongst local organizations throughout the Bay Area.

Join us for this fun and non competitive event (well, maybe just a bit competitive). Stay afterwards and take in the community spirit and camaraderie of the East Bay's oldest pride race.

Participants receive a pair of rainbow-colored EBFRW running socks. Don't miss out!
