ABOUT US

Who are we?

a running club. nonprofit.
in the San Francisco/Oakland Bay area.

Our Motto: Exercise. Have Fun!

Our Mission: Promote running and walking for the LGBTA community.

We meet on the east side of San Francisco Bay every Saturday morning at 9:00 AM. Distances range between 3-7 miles for runners and 2-4 miles for walkers.

We take pride in being friendly and inviting. ANYONE is welcome. Join us this weekend!

What we do


SATURDAY RUNS

Our running and walking events are non-competitive and self-paced. Runners complete between 3 to 7 miles, and walkers 2 to 4 miles. It usually lasts 45 minutes to one hour. All paths and trails are easily accessible to runners and walkers alike.

BRUNCH

We follow our exercise with brunch at one of several great local restaurants.
